Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

CashBFF ("we," "us") is operated by Khanna's LLC. This policy explains what we collect when you text us and what we do with it. We tried to keep it short and honest.

What we collect

  • Your phone number
  • What you text us (message content)
  • Credit card info you choose to share, like balances, number of cards, and how you usually pay (minimum, more than minimum, etc.)

Everything you share is self-reported. You text it to us in your own words. We only know what you tell us.

What we don't collect

  • Your actual credit card numbers
  • Bank account credentials or logins
  • Your SSN, date of birth, or any identity documents

We never connect to your bank. There's no Plaid, no account linking, nothing like that.

What we do with your info

We use it to give you personalized guidance on paying off your credit card debt over text. That's it.

What we never do

  • Sell or share your data with third parties
  • Share your info with advertisers
  • Report anything to credit bureaus
  • Give your phone number to anyone

How to delete your data

  • Text STOP at any time. We'll delete your data within 30 days.
  • For immediate deletion, email support@cashbff.com.

Text messaging

By texting CashBFF, you agree to receive text messages from us. You can text STOP at any time to opt out. Text HELP for support. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

Not financial advice

CashBFF is an educational tool. We are not financial advisors, and nothing we text you should be taken as professional financial advice. We're here to help you think through your debt, not to make financial decisions for you.

Data security

Your data is stored on encrypted servers in the United States. We use industry-standard security practices to keep it safe.

Anonymized data

We may use anonymized, aggregated data to improve the product. This means things like "X% of users pay minimums" and never anything tied to you individually.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll post the changes here with a new date.

Questions?

Email us at support@cashbff.com.
