why this shape

not a feature page. the LLM brain runs once a week on the back end. she only sees a single soft surface on home — the green card on stage 1.

review one at a time. ASD-respecting. one merchant per screen. confidence + reasoning visible. yes / no / edit. nothing else to think about.

uncertain ≠ shown to user. the high-confidence bucket is the only one that triggers a to-do. recurring_likely stays internal — surfaced quietly in the recurring tab if she ever opens it, but never as a prompt.

she stays in charge. approved patterns become real scheduled_txns rows — same shape as anything she added by hand. trash + edit work. running balance updates the second she taps yes.

under the hood (per the prototype):
last 90 days · grouped by normalized_merchant · ≥2 charges · one Sonnet 4.6 call with cache_control: ephemeral · cost $0.015–0.04 / sweep · weekly = ~$0.06 / user / month.
