terms.

the short, no-nonsense version. we're a tool that helps you see your money clearly. not financial advice, not a bank.

who can use cash bff

the regular product is for adults 18 and over. anyone under 18 can only use cash bff through our /school flow, which requires a parent or legal guardian to consent on their behalf. by signing up, you're confirming you meet one of these.

what we do for you

we sync your bank accounts via plaid (read-only. we never see your bank password), give you a calendar view of your money, help you schedule upcoming expenses, and detect your recurring bills and subscriptions so you can forecast cash flow. we're a tool that surfaces your own data back to you in a useful shape. your money, easier to see.

we do our best to keep things accurate, but every number you see comes from your bank's data, your own scheduled entries, or our forecasts on top of those. all three can be wrong sometimes.

what we don't do

we don't move money. we never initiate transfers, payments, or trades. we don't give regulated financial, investment, or tax advice. we don't guarantee any outcome. we surface your numbers, you make your own decisions.

we may use anonymized and aggregated data from how the product is used to make our forecasts smarter, our recurring detection more accurate, and our categorization better. we will never sell or share your raw data with anyone for any reason. for the long version, see our privacy policy.
